Comparing wind speed to latitude yields a bowl shaped overall look in the data. There seems to be cities with low wind speeds across the globe, both in the north and the south. However, overall there seems to be higher wind speed recording in the northern hemisphere. This helps visualize wind speeds across the globe and it looks like the sind speeds are highest in the southern hemisphere, in the middle of the oceans. However, since most of earth's landmass is in the northern hemisphere, this data may be a result of the bias that most cities are in the northern hemisphere.
